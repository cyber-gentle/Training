Fresh Garden Salad

This refreshing garden salad combines crisp vegetables and a light dressing, making it perfect for a quick and healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Cucumber pieces
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly
  2. Chop the vegetables into small pieces
  3. Place vegetables into a large bowl
  4. Drizzle olive oil and toss gently
